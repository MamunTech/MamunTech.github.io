East West University Robotics Club



Robotics is one of the branches of modern technologies with the design, control, operation and application of robots. EWU Robotics club works with new and updated/upgraded technologies and guides students to the exciting world of robots. It also helps to develop their problem solving skills, teamwork skills, creativity and technical knowledge in the field of robotics. This club motto is “Evolution of Dreams” directly reflects to the successful implementation of dreams using modern electro-mechanical software/hardware systems.





EWU Robotics club started its May in 2014 with the effort of few active students like Abdullah Al Mamun and his team members. It got official approval and registered under Career Counseling Center (CCC) of EWU in September 2016. At present, the club has more than 200+ active members.






The club is responsible to conduct various inter and intra robotics events, competitions and training activities through the year. It organizes semester long training courses related to robotics and other comparable disciplines and guides participants to apply their learning to assemble real world innovative projects. It also arranges seminars/workshops/invited speeches from nationwide distinguished speakers and creates opportunities for the students to gain vast knowledge on the subject of advanced technologies. The club facilitates to develop leadership and communication skills by building interconnection among past and present student members, students groups and industrial personnel.





Robotics club is open to all students in all disciplines. Students are cordially welcome to participate in different club related activities and explore them in robotics. Club members are encouraged and motivated to involve in national and international competitions and enhance university reputation.
